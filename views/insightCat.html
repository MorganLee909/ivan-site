Requirements Gathering

The goal is to streamline monitoring, eliminate the need for multiple products and accounts, and provide a user-friendly interface. Scalability is also a key focus, ensuring adaptability to business growth. The objective is to deliver an intuitive all-in-one monitoring solution that addresses these needs.
