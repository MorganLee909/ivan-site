Virtuozzo - Cloud Management Platform

Product Designer | UX/UI Developer

2022 - Present

Build for

Tools

Tech Stack

Virtuozzo, a leading software company specializing in virtualization and cloud management solutions, embarked on a transformative journey after acquiring OnApp and Jelastic. This strategic move propelled Virtuozzo into the realm of Multi-Cloud Platforms, offering a comprehensive suite of services to cloud computing providers, managed services providers, and internet hosting service providers.

As part of this evolution, I was brought on board to spearhead the interface and design system for Virtuozzo's new Cloud Management Platform. Tasked with integrating the functionalities of OnApp, Jelastic, and Virtuozzo into a unified solution, I navigated the project with resource constraints, leading user research, conducting competitor analysis, surveys, and usability tests.

Collaborating closely with the marketing team and business development, we meticulously crafted a range of essential features from the ground up. These included Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), Desktop as a Service (DaaS), Virtual Machines, Object Storage, User Management, Team Collaboration, Billing History, Billing Systems, Dashboards, Single Sign-On (SSO), and Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), all seamlessly interconnected through a robust Design System.

Furthermore, I curated a comprehensive component library using Storybook+Chromatic, ensuring consistency, scalability, and efficiency in design and development processes.

This case study dives deep into the challenges, strategies, and outcomes of our journey in transforming Virtuozzo into a powerhouse Multi-Cloud Platform, showcasing the pivotal role of design in driving innovation and market leadership.
